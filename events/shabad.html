Rules

  • TEAM SIZE: 1
  • Only one submission per person is allowed.
  • The poem is to be written only in Hindi, Urdu or combination of both languages.
  • The poem having at least 9 of the mentioned words will qualify as the legal participation. The used word(s) should strictly be underlined.
  • Use of star marked words will fetch extra points.
  • As this event is based mainly on the use of the mentioned words, so the priority will be given to the poem/poems using maximum of the listed words.
  • Judgment will be done on the basis of the use of the given words as per their appropriate meaning in the poem.
  • If any word from the list is used and its meaning is not clear in the context of the related subject, that particular word will not be counted.
  • Type, length and subject of the poem are independent.
  • In case, two or more members use the same number of words, decision will be taken on the close relation between the word and its used meaning in the poem.
  • Plagiarism in any form shall lead to immediate disqualification from the event.
  • The decision of the judges shall be final and binding.
