Youth Parliament

The Youth Parliament is a simulation of the Lok Sabha session of the Indian Parliament. Conducted in the exact same fashion, it provides participants a chance to become parliamentarians and experience the troubles and excitement of law makers with all the thrill and tension their job brings. The House consists of the Speaker, the Secretary General, the Marshall, the Government, and the Opposition. Participants may apply to become MPs from either the Government or the Opposition by filling the attached form on the Youth Parliament tab. The final allocation will, however, be determined by the organisers, essentially based on the responses filled in the form by the various participants. (Those who are not allocated any Ministry or Committee will sit in the house as ordinary MPs, but will be fully involved in all aspects of decision making.)
